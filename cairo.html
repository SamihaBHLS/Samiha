Experience Timeless Cairo

Where ancient pyramids meet bustling markets and the mighty Nile River

Pyramids of Giza

The last surviving wonder of the ancient world, including the Great Pyramid and Sphinx.

Must-do: Camel ride at sunset

Best time: Early morning to avoid crowds

Egyptian Museum

Home to the world's most extensive collection of pharaonic antiquities.

Highlight: Tutankhamun's golden mask

Tip: Hire a guide for best experience

Khan el-Khalili

Historic bazaar dating back to 1382, filled with spices, jewelry, and traditional crafts.

Must-try: Hibiscus tea at Fishawi's Cafe

Remember: Bargaining is expected

Nile River Cruise

Experience Cairo from the water with dinner cruises featuring traditional entertainment.

Popular choice: Evening dinner cruise

Tip: Book a boat with open-air deck

Islamic Cairo

Historic district with stunning mosques, madrasas and medieval architecture.

Must-see: Mosque of Muhammad Ali

Note: Dress modestly for mosques

Coptic Cairo

Ancient Christian area with churches dating back to the 3rd century AD.

Highlight: Hanging Church

Also see: Coptic Museum

Essential Travel Tips

Getting Around

Use Uber/Careem for taxis. Metro is efficient but crowded. Avoid driving yourself.

Money Matters

Egyptian Pound (EGP) is the currency. Carry small bills for tips and markets.

Weather

Hot summers (Jun-Aug), mild winters (Dec-Feb). Spring has sandstorms.

Cultural Norms

Dress modestly, especially women. Learn basic Arabic phrases. Haggle in markets.
