Experience Magnificent Mexico City

Where ancient pyramids stand beside colonial palaces and contemporary art

Zócalo

The massive main square, heart of the city since Aztec times.

Must-see: Metropolitan Cathedral and National Palace

Tip: Visit during the flag-raising ceremony at 8am

Teotihuacán

"City of the Gods" with massive pyramids just outside the city.

Highlight: Climb Pyramid of the Sun at sunrise

How to get there: 1-hour drive or guided tour

Frida Kahlo Museum

The Blue House where Frida Kahlo lived and created her art.

Must-do: Book tickets online in advance

Nearby: Coyoacán's charming plazas

Chapultepec Park

One of the largest city parks in the Western Hemisphere.

Must-see: Chapultepec Castle and Anthropology Museum

Best time: Weekday mornings for fewer crowds

Xochimilco

Colorful floating gardens with festive trajineras (boats).

Must-do: Hire a boat with mariachis

Tip: Try pulque (traditional fermented drink)

Palacio de Bellas Artes

Stunning art nouveau palace with Diego Rivera murals.

Must-see: The glass curtain stage

Best view: From Sears building cafe across the street

Best Restaurants

Pujol

World-famous modern Mexican cuisine by Enrique Olvera

Signature dish: Mole madre (aged 2,500+ days)

Contramar

Seafood institution famous for its tuna tostadas

Must-try: Pescado a la talla (whole grilled fish)

El Cardenal

Classic Mexican breakfasts and traditional dishes

Famous for: Hot chocolate and pan dulce

Los Cocuyos

Legendary late-night taco stand

Best for: Authentic tacos al pastor

Mexico City Wisdom

"CDMX is where ancient pyramids whisper secrets to modern skyscrapers, where the aroma of street tacos mingles with contemporary art galleries, and where every neighborhood tells its own colorful story."

Essential Travel Tips

Getting Around

Metro is efficient and cheap. Uber is safe and affordable.

Money Matters

Mexican Peso (MXN) is the currency. Carry small bills for markets.

Weather

Mild year-round (spring-like). Rainy season June-September.

Food Safety

Drink bottled water. Street food is safe at busy stalls.
