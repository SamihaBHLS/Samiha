Experience Beautiful Stockholm

Where Scandinavian design meets medieval charm and breathtaking archipelago views

Gamla Stan

Stockholm's charming Old Town with cobblestone streets, colorful buildings, and the Royal Palace dating back to the 13th century.

Must-see: Stortorget square

Best time: Early morning before crowds

Vasa Museum

Home to the almost fully intact 17th-century warship that sank on its maiden voyage, now painstakingly restored.

Unique fact: 98% original wood

Duration: Allow 2 hours

Skansen

The world's oldest open-air museum showcasing traditional Swedish life with historic buildings and Nordic animals.

Highlight: Christmas market in winter

Great for: Families

Archipelago Cruise

Explore Stockholm's stunning archipelago of 30,000 islands, islets and rocks stretching into the Baltic Sea.

Popular route: To Vaxholm

Best season: Summer months

ABBA Museum

Interactive museum celebrating Sweden's most famous pop group with costumes, memorabilia, and recording booths.

Must-do: Sing with hologram ABBA

Tip: Book ahead in summer

Fotografiska

World-class photography museum in a former customs house with stunning views from its top-floor restaurant.

Bonus: Great vegan restaurant

Open late: Until 11pm most nights

Essential Travel Tips

Getting Around

Excellent public transport. SL Access card works on all buses, metro, and ferries.

Money Matters

Swedish Krona (SEK) is the currency. Cards accepted almost everywhere.

Weather

Warm summers, cold winters. May-Sept best for outdoor activities.

Dining

Book restaurants in advance. "Dagens lunch" offers great value weekday lunches.
