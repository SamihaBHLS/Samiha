Experience Timeless Havana

Where pastel-colored facades, 1950s classics, and the soulful sounds of son cubano transport you to another era

Old Havana (Habana Vieja)

UNESCO-listed historic center with colonial plazas, baroque churches, and vibrant street life.

Must-see: Plaza Vieja & Cathedral Square

Tip: Follow the "Ruta del Café"

Malecón

Iconic 8km seawall where locals gather at sunset to fish, dance, and socialize.

Best time: Evening for golden hour

Don't miss: Street musicians

Classic Car Tour

Ride in a 1950s American convertible through Havana's most photogenic neighborhoods.

Duration: 1-2 hours

Price: $30-50 CUC

Fábrica de Arte Cubano

Havana's hippest art space with galleries, live music, bars and performance art.

Open: Thurs-Sun nights

Tip: Go early to avoid lines

El Floridita

Legendary Hemingway haunt famous for inventing the daiquiri cocktail.

Must-try: Papa Doble daiquiri

Note: Tourist prices apply

Fusterlandia

Colorful mosaic wonderland created by artist José Fuster in Jaimanitas neighborhood.

Distance: 20 mins from center

Tip: Wear bright colors for photos

Best Havana Eateries

Must-try: Lobster and mojitos

Essential Travel Tips

Money Matters

Dual currency system - tourists use CUC. Bring enough cash (cards often don't work).

Internet Access

WiFi only in parks/hotels via prepaid ETECSA cards. Embrace digital detox!

Nightlife

Don't miss live music at La Zorra y el Cuervo or Café Bertolt Brecht.

Day Trips

Visit Playas del Este beaches or Viñales valley for tobacco farms.
