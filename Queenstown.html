Experience Adventurous Queenstown

Where majestic mountains meet crystal lakes and adrenaline-pumping activities

Skyline Gondola

Breathtaking ride to Bob's Peak with panoramic views of Lake Wakatipu and The Remarkables mountain range.

Must-do: Luge rides at the top

Best time: Sunset for stunning views

Shotover Jet

Thrilling high-speed jet boat ride through the narrow Shotover River canyons with 360° spins.

Duration: 25 minutes of adrenaline

Tip: Prepare to get wet!

Milford Sound

World-famous fjord with towering cliffs, waterfalls and wildlife. Day trip from Queenstown.

Must-do: Scenic cruise

Travel time: 4hrs each way (worth it!)

Fergburger

Legendary burger joint with queues out the door - massive, delicious burgers worth waiting for.

Popular choice: The Ferg Deluxe

Tip: Order ahead by phone

Bungee Jumping

Birthplace of commercial bungee jumping at Kawarau Bridge (43m) or try the Nevis (134m).

For beginners: Ledge Swing first

Minimum age: 10 years

Arrowtown

Charming historic gold mining town with preserved buildings, great cafes and autumn colors.

Distance: 20 mins from Queenstown

Must-see: Chinese Settlement

Essential Travel Tips

Getting Around

Compact town center is walkable. Rent a car for day trips or use local buses.

Money Matters

New Zealand Dollar (NZD) is the currency. Cards widely accepted.

Weather

Four distinct seasons. Summer (Dec-Feb) warm, winter (Jun-Aug) for skiing. Pack layers.

Adventure Prep

Book popular activities in advance. Check weight/health restrictions for extreme sports.
