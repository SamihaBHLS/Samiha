Experience Dynamic Hong Kong

Where towering skyscrapers meet ancient temples and world-class cuisine

Victoria Peak

Hong Kong's highest point offering breathtaking panoramic views of the skyline and harbor.

Must-do: Peak Tram ride

Best time: Sunset for day-night transition

Star Ferry

Iconic ferry service across Victoria Harbour between Hong Kong Island and Kowloon.

Duration: 10-minute scenic crossing

Tip: Upper deck for best views

Temple Street Night Market

Vibrant night market with fortune tellers, street food, and bargain shopping.

Must-try: Claypot rice

Hours: 6pm-midnight

Tian Tan Buddha

Massive bronze Buddha statue on Lantau Island, accessible by scenic cable car.

Special: 268 steps to the platform

Combo: Ngong Ping Village

Hong Kong Disneyland

Magical theme park with unique attractions and the smallest, most manageable Disney experience.

Unique: Mystic Manor

Tip: Weekdays are less crowded

Lan Kwai Fong

Hong Kong's famous nightlife district packed with bars, clubs, and restaurants.

Best time: Thursday-Saturday nights

Atmosphere: International crowd

Essential Travel Tips

Getting Around

MTR is fastest way. Octopus card works for all transport. Taxis are affordable.

Money Matters

Hong Kong Dollars (HKD). ATMs everywhere. Credit cards widely accepted.

Weather

Hot and humid May-September. Best months are October-December.

Local Customs

English widely spoken. Exchange business cards with two hands. Don't tip.
