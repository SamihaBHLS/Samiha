Experience Paradise in Maldives

Where crystal-clear waters meet endless skies in the Indian Ocean

Overwater Bungalows

Iconic luxury accommodations built over the turquoise lagoon with direct access to the ocean below.

Best for: Romantic getaways & honeymoons

Feature: Glass floor panels to view marine life

Coral Reefs

Home to some of the world's most spectacular coral reefs with incredible biodiversity and vibrant marine life.

Activity: Snorkeling and diving

See: Manta rays, whale sharks, and turtles

Bioluminescent Beaches

Witness the magical natural phenomenon of glowing phytoplankton that illuminates the waters at night.

Best seen: On moonless nights

Location: Mudhdhoo Island's "Sea of Stars"

Malé

The bustling capital city with colorful buildings, historic mosques, and vibrant local markets.

Must-see: Islamic Centre and Grand Friday Mosque

Experience: Local culture and cuisine

Sandbank Picnics

Private picnics on secluded sandbanks in the middle of the ocean, accessible only by boat.

Experience: Ultimate privacy and romance

Feature: Disappears at high tide

Overwater Spas

World-class spa treatments in overwater pavilions with views of the ocean and sounds of gentle waves.

Specialty: Ayurvedic and thalassotherapy

Feature: Glass floor panels for marine views

Essential Travel Tips for Maldives

Best Time to Visit

November-April for dry season with less rainfall and calmer seas.

Getting There

International flight to Malé, then seaplane or boat to your resort.

Visa Requirements

30-day visa on arrival for most nationalities.

Currency

US dollars widely accepted. Maldivian Rufiyaa is local currency.
