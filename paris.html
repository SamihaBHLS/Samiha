Experience Romantic Paris

Where art, history, and joie de vivre come together in the most beautiful city

Eiffel Tower

Iconic iron lattice tower offering breathtaking views of Paris from its observation decks.

Must-do: Nighttime light show

Best time: Early morning to avoid crowds

Louvre Museum

World's largest art museum housing masterpieces like Mona Lisa and Venus de Milo.

Duration: At least half a day

Tip: Buy tickets online in advance

Notre-Dame Cathedral

Gothic masterpiece with stunning stained glass and gargoyles (currently under restoration).

Must-see: Rose windows

Nearby: Shakespeare and Company bookstore

Montmartre

Bohemian hilltop neighborhood with Sacré-Cœur Basilica and charming artist squares.

Popular spot: Place du Tertre

Tip: Take the funicular to save energy

Seine River Cruise

Romantic way to see Paris's landmarks illuminated at night from the water.

Best time: Sunset cruise

Duration: 1 hour

Palace of Versailles

Opulent royal palace with Hall of Mirrors and magnificent gardens (day trip from Paris).

Distance: 45 mins from Paris

Must-see: Marie Antoinette's Estate

Essential Travel Tips

Getting Around

Métro is efficient and affordable. Walk whenever possible to discover hidden gems.

Money Matters

Euro (€) is the currency. Many places accept cards but keep cash for small purchases.

Weather

Mild summers, chilly winters. Spring and fall offer pleasant temperatures and fewer crowds.

Cultural Tips

Always greet with "Bonjour" before asking questions. Dinner is typically after 8pm.
