Experience the Magic of Santorini

Whitewashed buildings, blue domes, and stunning sunsets await you

Oia Village

The most picturesque village in Santorini, famous for its sunset views and blue-domed churches.

Best time to visit: Early morning or late afternoon

Must-see: Sunset at the Byzantine Castle ruins

Fira

The vibrant capital of Santorini with shopping, nightlife, and breathtaking caldera views.

Don't miss: The cable car to the old port

Tip: Walk the scenic path from Fira to Firostefani

Red Beach

One of Santorini's most famous beaches with striking red volcanic cliffs.

Best for: Photography and swimming

Note: Can get crowded in peak season

Akrotiri

The ancient Minoan city preserved in volcanic ash, often called the "Greek Pompeii".

Hours: 8:00 AM - 8:00 PM (summer)

Admission: €12

Amoudi Bay

A charming fishing port below Oia with excellent seafood tavernas.

Try: Fresh octopus at Sunset Tavern

Adventure: Cliff jumping at the bay

Santo Winery

Sample Santorini's unique volcanic wines with spectacular caldera views.

Must-try: Vinsanto dessert wine

Tour: Wine tasting starts at €15

Essential Travel Tips

Getting Around

Rent an ATV for flexibility, or use the local bus system which connects major towns.

Best Time to Visit

May-June and September-October for pleasant weather and fewer crowds.

What to Wear

Comfortable shoes for cobblestone streets, light layers for warm days and cool evenings.

Photography Tips

Best light is early morning. Sunset spots fill up early - arrive at least 1 hour before.
