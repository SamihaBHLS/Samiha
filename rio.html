Experience Marvelous Rio

Where golden beaches meet lush mountains and vibrant culture

Christ the Redeemer

Iconic 98-foot statue atop Corcovado Mountain, one of the New Seven Wonders of the World.

Must-do: Sunrise visit for best views

Best way up: Cog train through Tijuca Forest

Sugarloaf Mountain

Granite peak with cable cars offering panoramic views of Rio and its beaches.

Highlight: Sunset from the summit

Tip: Combine with Urca neighborhood visit

Copacabana Beach

Legendary 2.5-mile beach with iconic wave-patterned sidewalk and vibrant atmosphere.

Must-try: Fresh coconut water from beach vendors

Best time: Early morning for jogging or sunset

Lapa Steps

Colorful mosaic staircase with 215 steps created by artist Jorge Selarón.

Nearby: Lapa neighborhood nightlife

Tip: Visit early to avoid crowds

Tijuca Forest

World's largest urban rainforest with waterfalls, hiking trails, and diverse wildlife.

Must-see: Cascatinha Waterfall

Activity: Guided jeep tours available

Maracanã Stadium

Legendary football stadium that hosted two World Cup finals and 2016 Olympics.

Experience: Attend a Flamengo match

Alternative: Stadium tour

Essential Travel Tips

Getting Around

Metrô is safest transit. Uber works well. Avoid unmarked taxis.

Money Matters

Brazilian Real (R$) is the currency. Cards widely accepted.

Weather

Summer (Dec-Mar) is hot/humid. Winter (Jun-Sep) is mild (20-25°C).

Safety

Leave valuables in hotel. Avoid flashing phones/cameras. Stick to tourist areas.
