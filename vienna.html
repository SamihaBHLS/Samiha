Experience Imperial Vienna

Where grand palaces meet musical heritage and coffeehouse culture

Schönbrunn Palace

Former imperial summer residence with 1,441 rooms and magnificent gardens.

Must-see: The Gloriette viewpoint

Tip: Book the Imperial Tour (22 rooms)

St. Stephen's Cathedral

Gothic masterpiece with colorful tiled roof and panoramic tower views.

Highlight: Catacombs tour

Best time: Evening organ concerts

Belvedere Palace

Baroque complex housing Klimt's "The Kiss" and magnificent gardens.

Must-see: Upper Belvedere art collection

Photo spot: Reflecting pool in the gardens

Vienna State Opera

One of the world's premier opera houses with stunning Renaissance Revival architecture.

Budget tip: Standing room tickets from €4

Season: September-June

Prater Park

Vienna's iconic amusement park with the giant Ferris wheel and green spaces.

Must-ride: Wiener Riesenrad (Ferris wheel)

Best time: Evening for city lights

Hofburg Palace

Former Habsburg winter residence, now presidential office and museums.

Highlights: Sisi Museum & Imperial Apartments

Nearby: Spanish Riding School

Essential Travel Tips

Getting Around

Excellent public transport (U-Bahn, trams, buses). Get a Vienna Card for discounts.

Money Matters

Euro (€) is the currency. Cards widely accepted but carry some cash.

Weather

Hot summers (25-35°C), cold winters (often below 0°C). Spring/fall are mild.

Culture Tip

Dress slightly formal for opera/concerts. Check for standing room tickets.
