Burj Khalifa

The world's tallest building at 828 meters with observation decks on 124th and 148th floors offering breathtaking views.

Must-do: Sunset visit for best views

Tip: Book tickets in advance online

Desert Safari

Experience dune bashing, camel rides, henna painting, and traditional Arabian entertainment under the stars.

Duration: 6-7 hours evening tour

Best time: October to March

Palm Jumeirah

Iconic artificial island shaped like a palm tree, home to luxury hotels including Atlantis The Palm.

Must-see: Aquaventure Waterpark

Viewpoint: The Pointe waterfront

Dubai Mall

World's largest shopping mall with 1,200+ stores, aquarium, ice rink, and spectacular fountain shows.

Highlights: Dubai Aquarium & VR Park

Fountain shows: Every 30 minutes after 6pm

Dubai Marina

Man-made marina district with stunning skyscrapers, luxury yachts, and vibrant nightlife.

Must-do: Dhow cruise dinner

Best time: Evening for cooler temperatures

Gold Souk

Traditional market in Deira with hundreds of retailers selling gold, diamonds and precious stones.

Tip: Bargain for best prices

Location: Near Al Ras Metro Station

Essential Travel Tips

Getting Around

Taxis are affordable. Metro is clean and efficient. Avoid driving unless familiar with local traffic.

Money Matters

UAE Dirham (AED) is the currency. Credit cards widely accepted but carry cash for souks.

Weather

Extremely hot May-Sep (40°C+). Best time to visit is Nov-Mar (20-30°C). Dress modestly.

Cultural Tips

Respect local customs - no public displays of affection. Cover shoulders/knees in traditional areas.
