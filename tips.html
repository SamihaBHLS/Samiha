Seasonal Travel Tips

  • Lightweight, breathable clothing
  • High SPF sunscreen and after-sun care
  • Hydration packs or water bottles
  • UV-protective sunglasses and hats
  • Quick-dry towels and swimwear

Winter Travel Must-Haves

  • Layered clothing for temperature changes
  • Quality thermal wear and accessories
  • Portable hand and foot warmers
  • Winterized gear for electronics
  • Lip balm and moisturizer for dry air

Spring Travel Tips

  • Pack for unpredictable weather
  • Allergy medication if needed
  • Light rain jacket or umbrella
  • Comfortable walking shoes
  • Camera for spring blooms

  • Versatile layers for cool mornings

  • Thermos for warm drinks
  • Extra batteries for shorter days
