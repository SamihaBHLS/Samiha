Common Visa Types

Tourist Visa

  • For leisure travel and sightseeing
  • Typically valid for 30-90 days
  • Usually no work permitted
  • Proof of return ticket often required

Business Visa

  • For meetings, conferences, negotiations
  • Typically doesn't permit employment
  • Often requires invitation letter
  • Shorter validity than work visas

Work Visa

  • Requires job offer or contract
  • Often tied to specific employer
  • May lead to permanent residence
  • Processing can take several months

Student Visa

  • Requires acceptance from institution
  • May allow limited part-time work
  • Proof of financial support needed
  • Valid for duration of study program

Visa Requirements by Region

European Visa Information

Schengen Area

26 European countries with border-free travel. Single visa covers all.

  • Tourist Visa: 90 days in 180-day period
  • Processing Time: 15-30 days
  • Fee: €80 for adults

United Kingdom

Separate visa required, not part of Schengen area.

  • Tourist Visa: Up to 6 months
  • Processing Time: 3 weeks
  • Fee: £95 standard

Eastern Europe

Some countries have different requirements than Schengen.

  • Examples: Russia, Belarus, Ukraine
  • Processing: Often requires invitation
  • Note: Check each country specifically

Asian Visa Information

Southeast Asia

Generally visa-free or visa-on-arrival for many nationalities.

  • Examples: Thailand, Vietnam, Indonesia
  • Duration: 15-30 days typically
  • Extensions: Often possible locally

East Asia

More stringent requirements for some countries.

  • China: Visa required in advance
  • Japan/Korea: Visa-free for many
  • Processing: 5-10 working days

South Asia

eVisa available for many countries.

  • India: eVisa for tourism
  • Duration: 30-90 days
  • Fee: $10-$100 depending

Visa Application Process

1

Determine Visa Type

Identify the correct visa category based on your travel purpose (tourism, business, study, etc.).

2

Gather Documents

Prepare passport, photos, financial proof, itinerary, and any required supporting documents.

3

Complete Application

Fill out the visa application form accurately and completely, either online or on paper.

4

Schedule Appointment

Book an appointment at the embassy/consulate or visa application center if required.

5

Submit Application

Attend your appointment, submit documents, provide biometrics if needed, and pay fees.

6

Wait for Processing

Processing times vary from a few days to several months depending on visa type and country.

Essential Visa Documents

Passport

  • Valid for 6+ months beyond stay
  • Minimum 2 blank pages
  • Undamaged and legible

Photos

  • Recent passport-style photos
  • Specific size requirements
  • Plain light background

Financial Proof

  • Bank statements
  • Sponsor letters if applicable
  • Minimum balance requirements

Accommodation

  • Hotel reservations
  • Invitation letters
  • Full itinerary details

Travel Itinerary

  • Round-trip flight tickets
  • Detailed travel plans
  • Tour program if applicable

Health Insurance

  • Required for some countries
  • Minimum coverage amounts
  • Valid for entire stay
